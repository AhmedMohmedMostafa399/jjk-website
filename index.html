Anime description

Jujutsu Kaisen is a dark fantasy action series following high schooler Yuji Itadori, who joins a secret organization of sorcerers to consume and contain the powerful curse, Ryomen Sukuna.
Renowned for intense animation by MAPPA and a unique power system, the series follows Yuji as he fights menacing Cursed Spirits.
